About NRProp

NRProp company is the manufacturer of propellers for Light, Ultralight, Experimental and Sport airplanes. We produce composite proppellers since 2003.

NRProp model list contains 20 various types Composite ground adjustable propellers and composite fixed pitch propellers for aircrafts, gyroplanes, paragliders, trikes, hovercrafts, autogyros, paraplanes and airboats etc. Most of them can produce in variant with 2, 3, 4 and 6 blades.

All our products designed using computer technologies and machined by CAD/CAM systems for highest quality and precision. Every new type propeller has been tested in a wind tunnel and passed damage tests.
