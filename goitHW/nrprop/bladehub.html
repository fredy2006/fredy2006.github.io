Blade Hubs

The hub is a base of the propeller. We produce hubs using aviation aluminum and anodise it then. The hub is machined on a milling machine without the participation of a person, which guarantees a high quality workmanship. The quality of the finished hub is checked using ultrasound. Such a hub has a large safety margin and provides a large service life of the propeller. Holes for fixing the hub to the engine allow the use of an air screw on engines of various types (Rotax, Lycoming and others)
